The Pullback Strategy

Stop guessing. Start following a process.

The Pullback Strategy gives you a clear framework for analyzing the market, waiting for confirmation, and executing only when every part of the setup is present.

Follow the checklist. Wait for confirmation. Execute with discipline.
01

Break of Structure

Identify a clear structural shift before considering an entry. The setup begins only after the market shows a meaningful break.

02

Fib the Move

Apply the Fibonacci tool to the move and mark the pullback area where price may offer a structured opportunity.

03

Drop to a Lower Timeframe

Refine the setup on a lower timeframe so you can read the price action more clearly and prepare for a precise entry.

04

Wait for Confirmation

Do not anticipate the move. Let price confirm your idea first. Patience protects you from entering an incomplete setup.

05

Breakout

Execute once the confirmation structure breaks, then manage the trade according to your predetermined risk plan.
